# How to change DNS settings on Android

<Callout type="info">
For a simpler setup, consider using [Private DNS (DNS over TLS)](/dot/android/) which is available on Android 11 and later.
</Callout>

<StepContainer>

### Open Network Settings

Go to `Settings` and select `Network & internet`:

[![Android settings menu](/images/guide/android/android-settings-menu.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-settings-menu.webp)

### Open Wi-Fi Settings

Tap `Wi-Fi`:

[![Android connections settings](/images/guide/android/android-connections-settings.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-connections-settings.webp)

### Access Network Details

Tap the gear icon next to your connected network:

[![Android WiFi settings](/images/guide/android/android-wifi-settings.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-wifi-settings.webp)

### Open Advanced Settings

Tap `Advanced`:

[![Android WiFi network details](/images/guide/android/android-wifi-network-details.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-wifi-network-details.webp)

### Note Your Current Settings

Take note of your current `IP address`, `Gateway`, and `Subnet mask`, then tap the pencil icon to edit:

[![Android IP settings static mode](/images/guide/android/android-ip-settings-static.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-ip-settings-static.webp)

### Configure Static IP

1. Change `IP settings` to `Static`
2. Re-enter your `IP address`, `Gateway`, and `Network prefix length`
3. Set the DNS servers:

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| DNS 1 | `185.222.222.222` |
| DNS 2 | `45.11.45.11` |

[![Android DNS server settings](/images/guide/android/android-dns-server-settings.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-dns-server-settings.webp)

4. Tap `Save`

[![Android DNS saved confirmation](/images/guide/android/android-dns-saved-confirmation.webp)](/images/guide/android/android-dns-saved-confirmation.webp)

</StepContainer>

<Callout type="warning">
The exact steps may vary depending on your Android device manufacturer and version. If this guide doesn't match your device, try searching for "change DNS settings" along with your device model.
</Callout>
